Children's Shelter of Cebu

They provide access to a unique community of services, giving children a new opportunity to live safe and healthy. For example ensured safety, warmth and kindness in their homes and health services to the children. Counseling gives children the chance to talk through the possibility of their adoption, letting them safely express their hopes and fears, and guaranteed safe adoption preparations if children are not able to return to their birth families, CSC makes sure that each child becomes eligible for adoption.
